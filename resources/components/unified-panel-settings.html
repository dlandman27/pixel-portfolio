Settings

A small toolbox for resetting progress or tweaking how the world behaves.

Reset All Items

Clears collected items and coins so you can re-discover everything from scratch.

Collision Debug View

Toggle a visual overlay that shows all collision lines and walls on the map. Helpful for tuning movement boundaries.

Center Camera on Dylan

If you've dragged the map around, use this to recentre the camera so Dylan is back in the middle of the screen.
